Welcome!

Barret Nobel, Founder of Barret's Restaurant, came to America hoping to be successful. His dream came true in 1987 when he opened Barret's Restaurant in Levittown, New York – a place to share his love and passion for authentic Italian food. His success was due in part by the use of his grandmother’s original recipes from Calabria, Italy and using only the highest quality imported ingredients. Over the years, people have come from all over Long Island, and more recently all over New York, to taste these old world recipes.

Through the years, Barret's Restaurant has expanded from a pizzeria and small dining area to a full catering facility with four rooms. Capable of holding private small and large functions of 35 to 150 people, Barret's specializes in: school events, fundraisers, Christenings, Communions, Confirmations & Bah Mitzvahs, Sweet Sixteen's and Weddings.

From Rich the Magish entertaining at your table on Sundays to Murder Mystery shows, Comedy shows, musical entertainment, and more, Barret's is the perfect restaurant for any occasion!
